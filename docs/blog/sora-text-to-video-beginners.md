## ✍️ 第一步：写出好的Prompt

Sora的核心技能就是写Prompt。与Midjourney不同，Sora需要的是"场景描述"而非"风格关键词"。

**❌ 错误的Prompt：**
```
a beautiful girl walking in the rain, cinematic, 4k
```

**✅ 正确的Prompt：**
```
A young woman walks slowly through a rainy Tokyo street at dusk. 
Warm light spills from a ramen shop onto the wet pavement, 
creating golden reflections. She holds a translucent umbrella, 
rain droplets slide off the edge in slow motion. 
Camera follows from a low angle, tracking her movement. 
Film grain, 35mm anamorphic lens, natural color grading.
```

关键差异在于：好的Prompt描述了**发生了什么、光线如何、镜头怎么动**，而不是堆砌形容词。

## 🎥 第二步：控制镜头运动

Sora支持自然语言镜头指令。在Prompt中嵌入这些关键词会显著提升输出质量：

- **推镜 (Dolly in)** — `Camera slowly pushes in toward the subject`
- **拉镜 (Dolly out)** — `Camera pulls back to reveal the wider scene`
- **摇镜 (Pan)** — `Camera pans left to follow the action`
- **升降 (Crane shot)** — `Crane shot rising above the cityscape`
- **跟拍 (Tracking shot)** — `Tracking shot following the character from behind`

实战技巧：在Prompt末尾加上 `Single continuous shot, no cuts` 可以避免Sora生成跳切画面。
